About Me

I work, I play, I cook, I draw, I bike, I dive, I wander, and I believe that our days may only be cloudy, rainy, or sunny but none of them could stop you from turning a normal day to an adventure while getting good stories and photos along the way.

I started as a Research and Development Specialist where I developed packaging materials that satisfies the needs of our customers, and evaluated the raw materials to ensure that they are suitable for the processes of our production lines. In July of the year 2022, I was made into a Process Engineer so I could give more attention to the standardization, optimization, and auditing of various processes in our manufacturing plant.
