Movie Recommendations

M. Noval

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

This movie totally caught me off-guard. Following its first film, I came into the cinema looking forward to a feel-good movie but found myself crying in just its first scene.

The Dark Knight

The trilogy, of which this film belongs to, is the best portrayal of batman so far - I appreciate how much realistic aspects has been applied to the the plot of these movies and the visual effects of the last 2 films. However, I hold The Dark Knight on a higher pedestal since it has some mind-blowing twists and Heath Ledger's iconic portrayal of The Joker.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2

I love the developments of the plot, along with the movies themes and humor.
